Common Questions

FAQ

Is this a subscription?

No. This is a one-time purchase for lifetime access to the 2.x product lifecycle. It is standard local software, not a SaaS service.

Do I need an OpenAI key?

No. NeuralShell is built for local-first privacy. It defaults to local model bridges (like Ollama). You can connect hosted APIs if you wish, but it is not required.

What OS is supported?

Currently, the V2.0 Alpha is a Windows-native executable. Mac and Linux support are on the roadmap for the Beta phase.

Who is this for?

Local-first AI users, privacy-minded operators, and advanced users who want a more serious, deterministic AI workflow shell than a browser-based chatbot.
