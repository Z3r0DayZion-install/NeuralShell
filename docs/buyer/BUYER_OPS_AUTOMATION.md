# BUYER_OPS_AUTOMATION

## Goal

Automate repetitive buyer follow-up operations without losing stage accuracy or technical truth.

## Stage Flows

1. Discovery follow-up.
2. Security-review packet trigger.
3. Procurement packet trigger.
4. Pilot-planning next-step guidance.

## Required Guarantees

- Recommendations must reflect actual current stage data.
- Generated follow-ups remain draft/review only.
- Recap and timeline outputs remain readable and exportable.
- No generic placeholder language and no unsupported claims.
