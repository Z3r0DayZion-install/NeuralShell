# STRATEGIC_ACCOUNT_ORCHESTRATION

## Goal

Operate strategic accounts with actionable stakeholder, deployment, blocker, and evidence mapping.

## Core Capabilities

- Strategic account map and deployment environment view.
- Stakeholder tracking with alignment and next-step fields.
- Active blockers and open risk management.
- Evidence-linked expansion hypotheses.
- Executive summary export for account reviews.

## Scope Guardrails

- No generic CRM filler fields.
- Expansion hypotheses must reference real deployment/pilot evidence.
- Executive brief remains concise and operational.
