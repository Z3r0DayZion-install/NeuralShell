# RENEWAL_RISK_CONSOLE

## Purpose

Provide a transparent renewal/retention risk view per account with concrete intervention guidance.

## Inputs

- Support load.
- Unresolved incident backlog.
- Deployment health.
- Adoption health.
- Executive sponsor confidence.

## Outputs

- Weighted risk score.
- Risk band classification.
- Intervention suggestions.
- Renewal summary export.

## Operating Rule

Risk scoring must remain explainable from explicit input factors; avoid opaque or synthetic scoring.
