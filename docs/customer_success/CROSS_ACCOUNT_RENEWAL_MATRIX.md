# CROSS_ACCOUNT_RENEWAL_MATRIX

## Goal

Manage renewal timing, risk, and intervention planning across multiple accounts in one view.

## Core Capabilities

- Renewal calendar matrix.
- Account-level risk flags and overlays.
- Support-load and deployment-health overlays.
- Intervention plan templates.
- Cross-account renewal pack export.

## Scope Guardrails

- Risk urgency must be easy to read.
- Interventions map to real account conditions.
- Export is concise and suitable for operations reviews.
