# NeuralShell First 3 Sales Milestone Plan

This document defines the learning and tightening objectives for each of the first three sales.

## 1. Sale #1: The Technical Validation
- **Objective**: Confirm the end-to-end "Buyer Experience" (Purchase -> License -> Discord -> Setup).
- **Metric**: Time-to-Setup. How long does it take from payment to their first successful "Operator" workflow?
- **Tighten**: Any friction points in the license entry or onboarding.
- **Learn**: What was the "One Feature" that closed the sale?

## 2. Sale #2: The Value Validation
- **Objective**: Confirm that the "Operator" tier value proposition ($99) is stable.
- **Metric**: Conversion Friction. Did the second sale require more "Selling" or less than the first?
- **Tighten**: The objection handling scripts in `docs/rc/NEURALSHELL_FIRST_10_MESSAGE_SYSTEM.md`.
- **Learn**: What questions did they ask that aren't in the FAQ?

## 3. Sale #3: The Fulfillment Validation
- **Objective**: Confirm that the "Manual Fulfillment" runbook is sustainable for 10-50 sales.
- **Metric**: Manual Fulfillment Overhead. How much "Founder Time" did it take to fulfill these three sales?
- **Tighten**: The `docs/rc/NEURALSHELL_FULFILLMENT_RUNBOOK.md` to make it faster/cleaner.
- **Learn**: Is the buyer joining the Discord automatically, or do we have to chase them?

---

## Post-Sale Audit (Per Sale)
1. **Identify**: Who bought? (Email, Profile)
2. **Track**: How did they find us? (Source)
3. **Verify**: Did they use the license key? (State)
4. **Discord**: Are they active in `#operator-support`?
5. **Feedback**: Ask for one thing that was "confusing" during the first hour.

**Milestone Reached**: Once the 3rd sale is closed, we have achieved **REVENUE_PROTOTYPE_STABLE**.
