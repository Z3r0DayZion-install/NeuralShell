# NeuralShell First 10 Execution Rhythm

This document defines the daily cadence for the first 10 buyer outreach conversations.

## 1. Day 1: The Initial Wave
- **Target**: Send all 10 initial messages (3 Warm, 4 Medium, 3 Cold).
- **Goal**: Initiate as many "conversations" as possible. Don't push for the sale on the first message for Medium/Cold prospects.
- **Log**: Fill in the `Initial Message` date in the `docs/rc/NEURALSHELL_FIRST_10_OUTREACH_TRACKER.md`.

## 2. Day 2-3: The Response Phase
- **Target**: Respond to all replies within 4-6 hours.
- **Goal**: Answer questions and provide the "Short Walkthrough" link.
- **Follow-up**: For prospects who haven't replied after 48-72 hours, send **Follow-up #1**.

## 3. Day 4-7: The Conversion Phase
- **Target**: Move interested prospects to the **Close**.
- **Goal**: Send the Gumroad link to anyone who says "Sounds great" or "I'm interested."
- **Objections**: Handle objections using the `docs/rc/NEURALSHELL_FIRST_10_MESSAGE_SYSTEM.md` logic.
- **Follow-up**: For those who haven't replied to Follow-up #1, send **Follow-up #2** on Day 7.

## 4. Week 2: Analysis & Next Batch
- **Analyze Outcomes**: Review the tracker for common objections or drop-off points.
- **Next Batch**: Only initiate a second batch (another 10 prospects) if the first batch hasn't yielded at least 1-2 sales. 
- **Goal**: Iterate the messaging or IFB profile based on the results of Batch 1.

---

## Daily Operating Checklist
- [ ] **Review Tracker**: Who is due for a follow-up today?
- [ ] **Check Channels**: Any new DMs or replies on X/Discord?
- [ ] **Fulfill Orders**: Any new sales from Gumroad? (Run the `Fulfillment Runbook`).
- [ ] **Update Tracker**: Log every interaction and state change.
