## Column Key & Guidance
- **Fit Level**:
  - **Warm**: High-trust, low-friction.
  - **Medium**: Right niche, moderate friction.
  - **Cold**: Right IFB profile, high friction.
- **Status Options**:
  - `Not Sent`
  - `Sent`
  - `Replied`
  - `Following Up`
  - `Objection Received`
  - `Closing`
  - `Closed (Sale)`
  - `Passed`
- **Follow-up Cadence**:
  - **1st Follow-up**: 2-3 days after the initial message.
  - **2nd Follow-up**: 5-7 days after the initial message.
- **Next Action**: Specific step to take (e.g., "Send demo link," "Invite to Discord," "Send Gumroad link").
