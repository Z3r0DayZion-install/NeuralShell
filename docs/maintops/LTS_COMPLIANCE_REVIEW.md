# LTS Compliance Review

## Ratified Policy
- Active Maintenance: 12 months from GM seal
- Security-Only Support: 24 months from GM seal
- End of Life: next major LTS baseline, or end of 24-month security-only window

## Monthly LTS Review Steps
1. Confirm current month offset from GM seal date.
2. Confirm support mode for current offset.
3. Review open items for mode compliance (feature vs maintenance).
4. Confirm communication requirements for compatibility or deprecation changes.
5. Record outcome and required actions.

## Mode Rules
- Active Maintenance window:
  - bug fixes, maintenance hardening, support improvements
- Security-Only window:
  - security and critical reliability fixes only
- End-of-Life transition:
  - prepare major baseline guidance and closure communication

## Output
- support mode confirmation
- exceptions list
- communication actions
