# REVENUE_OPERATIONS_CONSOLE

## Goal

Provide founder-grade revenue operations visibility tied to real imported/local account data.

## Core Capabilities

- Pipeline-to-revenue view.
- Won/lost/stalled summary.
- SKU and plan mix visibility.
- Expansion vs renewal split and partner-sourced signal.
- Monthly revenue operations pack export.

## Scope Guardrails

- No fake live-SaaS assumptions.
- No unsupported precision claims.
- Outputs stay aligned with commercial package structures.
