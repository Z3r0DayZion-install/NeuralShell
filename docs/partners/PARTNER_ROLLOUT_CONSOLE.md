# PARTNER_ROLLOUT_CONSOLE

## Goal

Provide a disciplined partner activation and readiness workflow after commercialization packaging is complete.

## Core Capabilities

- Partner activation checklist with owner/account context.
- Readiness scoring across assets, training, process, security, and activity.
- Partner blocker queue with sortable severity.
- Co-branded asset delivery visibility.
- Exportable partner handoff summary for internal and partner leads.

## Runtime Notes

- Console is local-first and export-only.
- No outbound automation is performed automatically.
- All readiness claims should map to concrete delivered assets or completed training records.
