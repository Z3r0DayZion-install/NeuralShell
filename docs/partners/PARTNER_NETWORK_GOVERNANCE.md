# PARTNER_NETWORK_GOVERNANCE

## Goal

Govern the partner network with defensible tier, compliance, and escalation states.

## Core Capabilities

- Network map and tier governance overview.
- Compliance and policy adherence summaries.
- Escalation, suspension, and reactivation state tracking.
- Exportable governance report.

## Scope Guardrails

- Governance transitions are logged and reviewable.
- Oversight outputs stay precise and evidence-backed.
