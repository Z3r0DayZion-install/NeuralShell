# PARTNER_CERTIFICATION_AND_ENABLEMENT

## Goal

Scale partner readiness with a formal certification and enablement program.

## Core Capabilities

- Certification paths by role with completion states.
- Enablement pack assignment and completion tracking.
- Co-sell readiness scoring and certified registry view.
- Recertification and expiry warnings.
- Exportable partner readiness pack.

## Scope Guardrails

- Registry states are evidence-backed and explainable.
- No unsupported offering is included in enablement assignments.
- Exports remain local-first and review-before-send.
