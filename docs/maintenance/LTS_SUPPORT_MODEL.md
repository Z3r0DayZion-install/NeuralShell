# NeuralShell V2.1.8 — LTS Support Model

## 1. Scope of Support
The NeuralShell V2.1.x line is designated for **Long-Term Support (LTS)** following the graduation of the V2.1.4 Golden Master.

| Component | Support Level | Commitment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Kernel** | High | Security & Integrity Patches only. |
| **Bridge** | Medium | Critical Compatibility fixes. |
| **CLI** | Low | Essential functional bugfixes. |
| **Docs** | Full | Ongoing stewardship maintenance. |

## 2. Support Horizon
- **Active Maintenance**: 12 Months from Golden Master Seal.
- **Security-Only**: 24 Months from Golden Master Seal.
- **End-of-Life (EOL)**: Upon release of the next major LTS baseline, or at the conclusion of the 24-month security-period.

## 3. Support Exclusions
- Feature requests or enhancements.
- Non-standard UI variations.
- Custom environment porting (Beyond documented baselines).
