# NeuralShell Reliability Deck (5 Slides)

## Slide 1 - The Failure Surface
- Startup failures block work before value starts.
- Drift creates inconsistent runtime behavior across environments.
- Teams lose time in long triage loops with weak operational evidence.

## Slide 2 - What NeuralShell Shows
- Startup health controls expose degraded startup paths early.
- Report-backed status highlights whether the environment is safe to proceed.
- Reliability owners get one readable control layer, not scattered logs.

## Slide 3 - What NeuralShell Isolates
- Plugin quarantine isolates unstable surfaces before blast radius grows.
- Teams can separate healthy startup paths from suspect runtime paths.
- Isolation improves recovery posture during incident handling.

## Slide 4 - What NeuralShell Exports
- Structured trace export for incident review and handoffs.
- Runtime report artifacts for reproducible debugging.
- Evidence chain supports release confidence gating decisions.

## Slide 5 - The 14-Day Pilot
- Baseline snapshot of startup reliability state.
- Controlled walkthrough of failure-to-confidence loop.
- Metric tracking through recovery and release decision points.
- Final recommendation: adopt, expand, or hold.
