# NeuralTube Monetization & Pricing Strategy

## 1. Watcher vs. Sovereign Positioning Copy

### NeuralTube Watcher (Prosumer & Media Consumers)
**"See how the story changes."**
NeuralTube Watcher is your personal narrative intelligence tool. Compare narrative framing across platforms like YouTube and Rumble side-by-side. Spot bias, drift, and selective framing instantly with LeanDetect overlays, and let our AI summarize transcripts so you can filter for what's actually "worth watching."

### NeuralTube Sovereign (Analysts & Investigators)
**"Prove what happened."**
NeuralTube Sovereign is an investigation-grade local analysis workstation. Designed for OSINT professionals, agencies, and researchers, Sovereign provides a structured narrative and evidence workflow. Export complete dossiers, manage cases, and run your analysis locally without leaking sensitive investigation context.

## 5. 90-Day Monetization Funnel

**Phase 1: Founder Beta (Days 1-30)**
*   **Goal:** Upfront cash generation and high-quality feedback.
*   **Action:** Direct outreach to `r/alphaandbetausers`, `r/osinttools`, and Indie Hackers. Drive traffic to an unlisted Gumroad link for the $99 Founder offer.

**Phase 2: Product-Led Growth (Days 31-60)**
*   **Goal:** Top-of-funnel volume and Pro conversions.
*   **Action:** Launch Watcher Free. Use organic search and social sharing of "narrative drift" screenshots to drive signups. Introduce the $12/mo Watcher Pro upsell for users hitting free tier limits.

**Phase 3: Institutional Sales (Days 61-90)**
*   **Goal:** Secure high-ticket MRR.
*   **Action:** Utilize the hardened, local-first architecture (Sovereign) to perform direct outbound sales to independent researchers (Solo - $499/yr) and small newsrooms/agencies (Team - $2,500/yr).

## 6. Checkout & Conversion Strategy

- **Visual Separation:** The pricing page strictly divides Watcher (light, accessible, fast checkout) from Sovereign (darker, serious, 'contact sales' or high-friction annual checkout).
- **The "Founder" Urgency:** The $99 one-time offer is presented as a limited-time opportunity before the $12/mo recurring model becomes the only paid option for Watcher.
- **Sovereign Friction:** Sovereign is positioned as a serious institutional buy. Purchasing requires a clear understanding of the "local-first" value prop, preventing casual consumers from accidentally buying the wrong tier.
